My Story

For several years I had been impacting people's lives through coaching. Influencing a person's development and experiences is very rewarding but I wanted to have an impact on a global scale. In addition to coaching, I worked on a company's website and quickly realized I wanted to have control on how things were displayed to the user - I was excited to discover the world of web development and began learning to code. More recently, I attended the Iron Yard Academy in Atlanta, GA - a full-time front end development program - to enhance my code base and ensure I stayed current with the latest technologies. I've always had a passion for technology and developing or enhancing web applications has enabled me to not only reach more people but to also continue to influence experiences.

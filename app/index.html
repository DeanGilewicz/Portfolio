My Story

Over the past few years, I have been impacting people's lives through coaching. Influencing a person's development and experience is very rewarding but I wanted to have an impact on a global scale.

I've always had a passion for technology so this was a natural path for me to pursue to not only have a larger impact on the world via web apps but to have an influence on a user's experience.

In addition to coaching, I worked on the company's website and quickly realized I wanted to have control on how things were displayed to the user - I was excited to discover the world of web development and code and began learning through online resources.

More recently, I attended the Iron Yard Academy in Atlanta, GA - a full-time front end development program - to enhance my code base and ensure I stayed current with the latest technologies.
